ACME Corp — Customer Policy Guide
==================================

RETURN POLICY
-------------
Items purchased from ACME Corp may be returned within 30 days of the original
purchase date. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its
original packaging, and accompanied by a valid receipt or proof of purchase.

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days
of receiving the returned item. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the
return is due to our error.

To initiate a return, contact our support team at returns@acme-corp.example.com
or call 1-800-555-0199. You will receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA)
number that must be included with your shipment.

PAYMENT METHODS
---------------
We accept the following payment methods:
- Visa, MasterCard, and American Express credit and debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank wire transfer (for orders over $500)
- ACME gift cards

All transactions are processed in USD. International orders may be subject to
currency conversion fees charged by your bank.

Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.

Email: support@acme-corp.example.com
Phone: 1-800-555-0100
Live chat: Available on our website during business hours

For urgent technical issues, our premium support line (1-800-555-0911) operates
24/7 and is available to customers with an active maintenance contract.

SHIPPING POLICY
---------------
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days within the continental United States.
Expedited shipping (2–3 business days) is available for an additional fee.
Overnight shipping is available for orders placed before 2 PM EST.

Free standard shipping is offered on all orders over $75. Orders under $75 incur
a flat shipping fee of $6.99.

International shipping is available to over 40 countries. Delivery times and
costs vary by destination. Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of
the recipient.

PRIVACY POLICY
--------------
ACME Corp collects personal information solely for the purpose of processing
orders and providing customer service. We do not sell, rent, or share your
personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data is stored securely and encrypted at rest and in transit. You may request
deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting privacy@acme-corp.example.com.
